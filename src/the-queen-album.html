The Queen Album

On the 27th of September Robby will perform a full band performance at the QUEEN CONVENTION in Prestatyn. For the occasion he combined his two first QUEEN tribute albums and also recorded new QUEEN songs to complete The Queen Album.

On the 13th of September, this album, containing (more than) 22 songs will be presented in 'CAPSLOC' in Capelle aan den IJssel, in Holland.

To all people who can't attend the QUEEN CONVENTION in Prestatyn this year, this is your chance not to miss out on something big.
